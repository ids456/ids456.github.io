Calculate kW Consumption:


Many customer undersetsimate importance of A/C maitenance. Proper maintenance ensures that air conditioning system operates at peak efficiency. Clean filters, coils, and other components allow the system to function optimally, reducing energy consumption and ultimately lowering your utility bills. Based on the size of the unit, on average A/C unit consumes 1.2-6.5 kW per hour of power. There is a formula below to calculate amount of money you will have to pay when your A/C is on for an hour.Detailed instruction provided below:


  • Enter amount of kW your A/C unit consumes every hour
  • Enter electricity cost (per kW)
  • Press 'Calculate'
  • The cost will appear in Popup Box
